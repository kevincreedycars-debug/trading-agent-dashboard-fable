Overview Definitions

What the numbers mean

DirectionThe model's current directional call for the asset and timeframe.
ConfidenceThe headline quality score for the call. It combines evidence dominance, participation, net edge, missing data, event risk, and timing filters. It is not a guaranteed probability.
Bull CaseThe percentage of active directional evidence supporting an upward or bullish call.
Bear CaseThe percentage of active directional evidence supporting a downward or bearish call.
Net EdgeThe gap between bull evidence and bear evidence. A wider gap means the active evidence is less conflicted.
ParticipationHow much of the model had usable directional evidence. Low participation means the call is based on fewer active inputs.
StrengthA descriptive label derived from confidence, net edge, and participation.
No CallThe model did not have enough clean evidence, participation, or market conditions to issue a directional call.
